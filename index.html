Who We Are

About Our Ministry

The Global Ministry of Jesus Christ Inc is a community dedicated to spreading the gospel, nurturing spiritual growth, and serving humanity with compassion and purpose.

📖

Our Mission

To proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ, make disciples of all nations, and demonstrate God's love through meaningful service and authentic relationships.

🕊️

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation, building a global community of believers who impact their world with the message of salvation and grace.

❤️

Our Values

We are grounded in faith, united in love, committed to truth, and passionate about serving God and humanity with integrity and excellence.
